Error of MCMC Approximation To Integral Over All 2 Qubit Quantum States

The integral over all 2 qubit quantum states is defined by a certain Haar integral over the unitary group. This integral was approximated using the Metropolis-Hastings algorithm to produce a sample of unitary matrices that are approximately Haar distributed.

The Average Purity Of Uniformly Distributed Density Matrices

Density matrices tend to concentrate tightly around their mean, the maximally mixed state, which is the quantum version of the uniform distribution. This means that uniformly chosen density matrices tend be far away from pure quantum states. Similar concentration of measure phenomena occur frequently in quantum computing.

Numerical Simulation Of Classical And Noisy Lorentz Systems

This plot shows the trajectories of both the classical and noisy Lorentz systems, approximated using the Euler method. Notice how just a small presence of noise is enough to completely change the trajectory of a particle in chaotic systems.
